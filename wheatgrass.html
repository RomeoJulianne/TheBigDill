Click Me → Wheatgrass seeds are called
"Hard Red Wheat Berries"
White Wheat berries, (soft), are a dirivitive
and are better for baking,
so look for red to grow wheatgrass.

For a 6-8" pot, place 2oz of wheatberries
in 4oz of distilled or filtered water

  • Soak for 8-12 hours


    • In my experience, more than 12 hours
    creates mold and kills the wheatgrass when it is young.
    Spread evenly in potted organic soil and cover slightly. Sprouts will emerge after 2-3 days
    Wheatgrass will be full grown in about a week,
    although your cat will probably start mowing it down
    as soon as she can! The fiber in wheatgrass is excellent
    for digestion and helps break down hairballs.
    **Wheatgrass kills cancer cells**         		Grow enough for yourself!
    Juice or enjoy raw in salads
    For a steady supply, begin a
    new batch from soak every 10 days.
